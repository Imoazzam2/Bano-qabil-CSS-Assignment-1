Career Pathways in Science Education

Whether you're just starting your career or looking to make a change, there are many paths to working in science education and outreach.

Education & Curriculum

Roles focused on developing educational content, designing learning experiences, and training educators.

  • Curriculum Developer
  • Educational Designer
  • Teacher Trainer
  • Learning Specialist

Technology & Digital Media

Positions involving the creation of digital tools, platforms, and media for science education.

  • VR/AR Developer
  • UX/UI Designer
  • Multimedia Producer
  • Software Engineer

Research & Evaluation

Roles focused on studying learning outcomes, evaluating programs, and conducting educational research.

  • Research Scientist
  • Evaluation Specialist
  • Data Analyst
  • Assessment Developer
