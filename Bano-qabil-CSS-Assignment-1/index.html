Welcome to the Chemistry Museum

The Virtual Museum of Chemistry houses a collection of instruments, books and memorabilia that showcase the evolution of chemical science. Among other things, it offers visitors an insight into how scientific discoveries have shaped our world, and to see historical scientific instruments, rare chemical samples, and interactive exhibits that bring chemistry to life.

Check out our Virtual Exhibits Catalog to explore our collections.

Our virtual doors are open 24/7. All exhibits include detailed information with links to additional resources. We offer interactive learning experiences and virtual tours.
